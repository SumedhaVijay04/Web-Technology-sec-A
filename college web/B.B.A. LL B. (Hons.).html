Course Overview


>> Five-year Integrated B.B.A LL .B. (Hons.): We are witnessing an unprecedented growth of start-ups in
India and a budding culture of entreprenuership. The integrated course in business administration and
law aims to cater to the need of such times. Apart from gaining the knowledge of law, students will
also be learning about the contemporary issues in business. This will be a valuable asset in today’s
growing market. Major corporate houses, law firms, multinational companies, start-ups
particularly value these skills.












Eligibility

>> 10+ 2 or equivalent with minimum 45% marks in aggregate from a recognized board.







Age Limit

- For General category: 20 years.

- For SC/ST & Other Backward Communities: 22 years.








Course structure

- The course structure will follow what has been prescribed by the Bar Council of India (BCI).

– B.B.A. LL. B. (Hons.) students will study Management, Accountancy, Finance, besides the Law Courses mandated by BCI.
