Course Overview


>> The MA programme in Economics at RV University is envisaged in a way that builds a strong foundational
understanding of Economics while at the same time preparing students to enter into professional careers in
their chosen field among multiple fields of economics. The programme starts with Core Courses in Economics
that builds the disciplinary foundations and provide students with a rigorous understanding of economic
theories, principles and their applications. The Core Courses prepare students to think like an economist. On
completion of core courses, students’ progress to choose their elective courses. The elective courses
familiarise students with the rich and multiple fields and discourses in economics where students can test their
theoretical understandings on empirical settings and contemporary or historical realities. These courses
prepare students to analyse and interpret the world through the economists’ lens.

>> In the final year, students can choose a specialisation track. Each specialisation track is designed to prepare
students to embark on a career in a specific field in economics. Research and industry experience forms an
important component of the MA programme. Students complete a research project and gain industry
experience in their chosen field of specialisation. Thus, through specialisation and research & industry
experience. students learn to be an economist in the area of their choice. Students who wish to continue their
education can seamlessly enter into the doctoral programme in economics offered by the school.

>> This natural and effortless progression of the students’ learning journey from thinking to being an economist is
the unique feature of the MA Economics curriculum at RVU. Students can gradually build their understanding,
make an informed choice about their areas of professional expertise, and acquire skills and capabilities to hone
their abilities to spread their wings in the real world.
