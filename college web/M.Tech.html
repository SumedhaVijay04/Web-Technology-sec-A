M.Tech (Data Science)





Programme Overview


>> Data Science is a field that gives valuable insights from unstructured data points, using various scientific
methods and algorithms. It is interdisciplinary and combines the fields of mathematics, statistics, artificial
intelligence, computer science and different domains and helps in creation of forecasts, and data-driven
decisions. In our program, students learn from expert faculty and industry professionals, in collaboration with
upGrad Campus, and solve real-world problems in their domains, via capstone projects, case studies or thesis
work. They also receive extensive career support and placement assistance to start and accelerate their career.














Certifications

>> At the end of the program, “M.Tech in Data Science” degree certificate will be awarded by
RV University.

>> At the end of the program, “Data Science” course completion certificate will be awarded jointly by upGrad
Campus and RV University.

>> If a student should exit without completing the program in its entirety, the following certifications will be
awarded. The corresponding credits will be accounted for in the Academic Bank of Credits that they
can use later towards their M.Tech. degree, as per the New Education Policy (NEP).

>>After successful completion of the first year.
- Certificate in Data Analysis.

>> After successful completion of the first two years.
- Advanced Post Graduate Diploma in Data Analysis and Machine Learning.

>> Micro Masters by upGrad Campus.
- Python for Data Science

- Deep Learning
- Specializations, if any
